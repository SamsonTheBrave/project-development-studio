Trailz with a z

, a fork in the path. Cole, and I have a tendency to talk a lot. This is great because we are constantly refining, remixing and retelling our ideas. That said, our tendency towards talking means that it's only now (admittedly late in the game) that I have an update for the trail summit. Instead of focusing on where we've been, Trailz, is now focusing on where we are. "Very mindful of you." Thanks. We know. We arrived here because of a few things, but primarily because we realized that the way we were intending to visualize the "trailz" were in the form of a branching tree structure, which seems great initially, but when it comes down to it, it seemed like too rigid a form. Forcing user data to conform to a tree structure is both difficult and likely to result in bad user interaction. Specifically, it would likely require them to manage their own branches and paths. Gross! By focusing on the present we do not have to worry to much about how to display a users history, we just have to worry about serving up relevant bookmarked content to the site they are on. Fortunately, this doesn't derail our efforts thus far. We can still use much of the framework for the database that we have created and the chrome extension. The biggest casualty of this is the metaphor of a trail, doesn't make the most sense because we are now focusing more on content and context of the current page. I think we're close to the end for our friend at the end of the alphabet.
